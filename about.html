EST

The goal of the EST is to allow you to practice "small-talk"-type conversations. At each stage of the conversation, the EST gives you
a relevant guideline you can use in real conversation.

We came up with the social guidelines for the EST using:

  • Social Skills for Teenagers and Adults with Asperger Syndrome: A Practical Guide to Day-to-day Life by Nancy J Patrick
  • Improve Your Social Skills by Daniel Wendler
  • Social Skills for Teenagers with Developmental and Autism Spectrum Disorders: The PEERS Treatment Manual by Elizabeth A. Laugeson and Fred Frankel
  • Social Skills Training for Children and Adolescents with Asperger Syndrome and Social-Communications Problems by Ted Baker
