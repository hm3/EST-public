The goal of casual conversation is to make people more comfortable around each other.
If people are comfortable with each other, they're more open to conversation as the rapport between them increases.
Therefore, generally, you want to avoid controversial or highly-specific topics until you have a higher level of rapport.
This simulation consists of 2 classmates talking one morning before class, and assumes the conversants only know
each other casually, so for now you are only able to "discuss" benign topics.
